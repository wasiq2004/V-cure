END THE DISCOMFORT

Expert Management of
Impacted Teeth

Impacted wisdom teeth can cause severe pain, swelling, and damage to adjacent molars. At V Cure Dental, our oral surgeons use advanced imaging to plan the extraction path, ensuring that the surrounding bone and nerves are protected throughout the procedure.

  • Painless local anesthesia techniques
  • Prevention of crowding and cyst formation
  • Precision 3D Digital X-rays for planning
