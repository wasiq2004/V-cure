METAL-FREE STRENGTH

Zirconia Crowns

Combining extreme durability with natural translucency for a restoration that lasts a lifetime.

PREMIUM PROTECTION

Strength Meets
Natural Beauty

Zirconia is a 100% metal-free, biocompatible ceramic material known as "Ceramic Steel." At V Cure Dental, we provide Zirconia crowns that are custom-milled to match the exact shade and shape of your surrounding teeth, providing a seamless look that traditional metal-fused crowns cannot achieve.

  • No "dark metal line" at the gum line
  • Highly resistant to chipping or cracking
  • Gentle on opposing natural teeth
WHY CHOOSE ZIRCONIA?

Advantages of All-Ceramic

Biocompatible

Zirconia causes zero allergic reactions or inflammation in the gums, making it the healthiest choice for your body.

Thermal Stability

Unlike metal, Zirconia does not conduct heat or cold, significantly reducing tooth sensitivity after placement.

Lifelike Translucency

The material mimics the light-reflecting properties of natural enamel, providing an indistinguishable restoration.

GERMAN ENGINEERING

Digital CAD/CAM
Precision Milled

We utilize Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM) to ensure your crown fits with sub-micron accuracy. By digitally scanning your tooth, we eliminate the need for messy impressions and deliver a crown that aligns perfectly with your bite.
