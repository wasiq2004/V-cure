Zirconia Crowns
Strength Meets

Zirconia crowns are advanced, metal-free dental caps made from Zirconium dioxide, one of the strongest and most durable materials used in modern dentistry.Zirconia combines exceptional strength with natural tooth-like appearance, making it one of the most preferred choices of crowns today.

Care Tips

Advantages of Zirconia

Tooth preparation

Tooth preparation involves removing decayed tissue, shaping enamel and dentin to support restorations, ensuring retention, resistance, and durability.

Digital scan / impression

Digital scan captures accurate three dimensional images of teeth using intraoral scanner, replacing traditional impressions, improving precision, comfort, efficiency.

Crown design using CAD-CAM technology

Crown design using CAD-CAM technology creates precise digital restorations, enhancing fit, strength, esthetics, reducing fabrication time and errors. .
