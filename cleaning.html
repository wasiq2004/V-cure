ORAL HYGIENE FIRST

Cleaning & Polishing
Brushing

A healthy smile begins with clean teeth and healthy gums. Even with regular brushing and flossing, plaque and tartar can build up in areas that are difficult to clean at home.Professional dental cleaning and polishing is a treatment that removes plaque, tartar, and stains — helping you maintain strong teeth and fresh breath.A cleaning appointment needs to be on everyone’s calendar, once every six months. It’s also a great opportunity to catch issues like cavities or loose teeth very early, before they become a bigger issue.Good Oral hygiene helps to maintain good general health. Poor oral hygiene leads to severe dental and medical problems. Regular dental cleaning helps to prevent these problems and gives you brighter smile.

  • Removes stubborn coffee, tea, and tobacco stains
  • Prevents Gingivitis and Periodontitis
  • Eliminates the source of bad breath
OUR HYGIENE PROTOCOL

A Multi-Step Refresh

Bleeding gums

Bleeding gums often indicate gingivitis, plaque buildup, vitamin deficiency, aggressive brushing, or infection.

Puffy and swollen gum

Swollen gums may result from gingivitis, infection, hormonal changes, injury, or poor hygiene.

Bad breath

Bad breath may result from poor oral hygiene, infections, dry mouth, or diet.
