Cleaning & Polishing

Restore your oral health and brighten your teeth with our professional ultrasonic scaling services.

ORAL HYGIENE FIRST

Beyond Regular
Brushing

Even with perfect brushing, plaque can harden into tartar (calculus) that only a dentist can remove. At V Cure Dental, our professional cleaning removes these deposits, preventing gum disease, bone loss, and tooth decay while leaving your mouth feeling incredibly fresh.

  • Removes stubborn coffee, tea, and tobacco stains
  • Prevents Gingivitis and Periodontitis
  • Eliminates the source of bad breath
OUR HYGIENE PROTOCOL

A Multi-Step Refresh

Ultrasonic Scaling

We use high-frequency vibrations to gently "shake" tartar off your teeth without damaging the enamel. It's fast, effective, and painless.

Prophy-Polishing

After scaling, we use a special prophy-jet or polishing paste to remove surface stains, making your teeth look naturally whiter.

Gum Protection

By removing bacteria from beneath the gum line, we allow your gums to heal and reattach firmly to your teeth, preventing tooth loss.

PREVENTIVE CARE
