Dental Implants
A Natural Smile

A Dental implant (Artificial tooth root) is the best treatment known to replace a lost tooth. An implant is a screw-like material made of titanium that replaces the root portion of a natural tooth. It is placed inside your jawbone by surgery over which crowns, dentures or bridges are fixed. If placed under proper conditions and further maintained diligently, an implant can last for a lifetime.

  • Replace one or more teeth without altering the adjacent teeth like dental bridges
  • Dental Impants prevent jaw bone loss or facial sagging due to missing tooth
  • Dental implants provide permanent and stable tooth replacement
STEP OF DENTAL IMPLANTS

Dental Implants are Placed

Step 1

Thorough clinical examination is made and Dental X-rays are taken and if required a CBCT scan will be advised.

Step 2

Implant placement is carried out as minor oral surgery by raising a flap or flapless (key hole) after numbing the area by local aesthesia.

Step 3

Implant is placed in the jaw and is left to bond with the bone and to heal completely. This takes minimum of 3 to 4 months.

Step 4

In the meanwhile, temporary crowns or dentures may be provided.

Step 5

Once Osseo integration (Bone-Implant junction) is formed, the permanent crown or Bridge which is to be replaced is fitted over the Implant.

Step 6

After Implant placement post operative care and follow up is must. By maintaining good oral hygiene and having regular check-up a good integrated Dental Implant can lasts a lifetime.

OUR SPECIALIZED SERVICES

Types of Implant Solutions

Single Tooth Implant

Replaces one missing tooth in a fixed and secure way. It looks, feels, and functions exactly like your natural tooth, preventing bone loss and preserving your beautiful smile.

Multiple Tooth Implants

Restore several teeth while maintaining absolute chewing function and jaw health. There's no need to grind down adjacent healthy teeth as required by traditional bridges.

All-on-4 / Full Mouth

Full-arch restoration (upper or lower jaw) using just 4 strategically placed implants. Often completed in a few days, giving you a completely brand new, permanent smile.

Zygomatic / Pterygoid Implants

Anchored deep in the cheekbone, these are the ultimate solution for patients who have suffered severe upper jaw bone loss and were previously told they could not have implants.

Keyhole Implants

A high-precision, minimally invasive placement technique. Keyhole surgery means no incisions or stitches, resulting in incredibly fast healing and zero downtime.

Mini Implants

Ultra-thin implants that are fantastic for stabilizing loose dentures or fitting into narrow spaces where standard implants simply cannot be placed due to limited bone.

TAD Implants

Temporary Anchorage Devices (TADs) are tiny temporary implants used exclusively for precise tooth movement during complex braces or aligner orthodontic treatments.

Key Aspects of Keyhole Implants

Procedure

A small, circular, "keyhole" incision is made in the gum tissue rather than a large, flap-like incision.

Minimally Invasive

Because no large flaps are raised, there is less tissue trauma, bleeding, and post-surgical pain.

Technology-Driven

We use CBCT scans and guided technology for precise, safe placement of implants where required.

Duration

The procedure is generally faster than traditional methods, often requiring only one, 30-minute session.

Recovery

Quick healing, typically with little to no swelling.
